Types of Crime Fluctuate Between Districts from 2015 to 2019

What types of crimes occur within specific Boston neighborhoods? How has this changed since 2015? Select your neighborhood from the dropdown below to see how the number of crimes, separated by type, has changed in the past years. The bubbles will shift to compare these numbers.

The bubbles reveal that Assaults are most common in all neighborhoods, followed by Vandalism. For many neighborhoods, such as Roxbury, Mattapan, and South End, the total number of crimes skyrocketed between 2015 and 2016. Roxbury in particular had the greatest number of crimes, especially in less common crime categories such as Homicides and Sex Crimes.
